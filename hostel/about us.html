The location of Govt. Eklavya Adarsh Vidiyalay Karpawand is Bakawand, Bastar. 5 dedicated and professional teachers ensure that the children get the maximum out of their eduaction in this Other Board school. In 2005, Govt. Eklavya Adarsh Vidiyalay Karpawand was launched. This school's primary medium of instruction is Hindi with Sanskrit and English as secondary medium and the student teacher ratio is 36:1. The school tries to provide best learning environment. Classes from 6th to 12th run in this school. 420 is approximately the current student strength of this school with residential facilities. It has a library as well with over 400 books. The school has given exceptional results in the academic sphere and its students have excelled in extra co-curricular activities too.
